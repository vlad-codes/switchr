Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Overview

Switchr is a browser extension that runs entirely on your device. It does not transmit any data to external servers and does not collect any personal information.

2. Data stored locally

Switchr stores the following data exclusively in your browser using the Chrome Storage API (chrome.storage.local):

  • Account slot names (labels you choose)
  • Twitch session cookies associated with each slot
  • The ID of the currently active slot

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to any third party, including the developer of Switchr.

3. Cookies

Switchr reads and writes Twitch session cookies in your browser in order to switch between accounts. These cookies are issued by Twitch and governed by Twitch's Privacy Notice. Switchr does not create, share or sell any cookies of its own.

4. Analytics & tracking

Switchr contains no analytics, crash reporting, telemetry, or any other form of tracking.

5. Permissions used

  • cookies — to read and restore Twitch session cookies
  • storage — to persist slot data locally
  • tabs — to reload Twitch tabs after switching accounts
  • host_permissions (twitch.tv) — to access cookies scoped to twitch.tv

6. Third-party services

The landing page (this website) loads the JetBrains Mono font from Google Fonts. This request is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. The extension itself does not make any external network requests.

7. Your rights

Since Switchr stores no personal data on any server, there is nothing to request, correct or delete from our side. All data is under your full control — you can clear it at any time by removing the extension or clearing extension storage in your browser settings.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, open an issue in the GitHub repository.
